Before moving on. I want to acknowledge that folding a smartphone might not be for everyone.

If you don't feel addicted to your phone and you are happy with how you are using it, this lifestyle might not be for you.

Furthermore, folding your phone is not a "get rich quick" type of scheme. By following the steps of this method, it's not a easy cop out way for you to be happier. Only what you put into it is what you get out of it.
