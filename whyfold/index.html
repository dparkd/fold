Why should you fold your phone?

A lot of us are addicted to our phones. However, I believe that the phone in and of itself is not a bad thing to have or use. It only becomes a problem when the phone is no longer a tool that we utilize but one that we submit to.

The phone should make travel easier (lyft, google maps, waze), finance faster (venmo, banking apps) and life better (meditation, notes)

The phone should not make us use time aimlessly (candy crush, pokemonGO), make us addicts to infinite content (facebook, instagram, snapchat) and make us slaves to our work (slack, gmail)
